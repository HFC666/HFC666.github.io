Vector derivative identities

  1. $\nabla\times \nabla f=0$
  2. $\nabla\cdot(\nabla\times u)=0$
  3. $\nabla \times(\nabla \times u) =\nabla(\nabla \cdot u)-\nabla^{2} u$
  4. $\nabla \cdot(f u) =u \cdot \nabla f+f \nabla \cdot u$
  5. $\nabla \times(f u) =\nabla f \times u+f \nabla \times u$
  6. $\nabla(u \cdot v) =(u \cdot \nabla) v+(v \cdot \nabla) u+u \times(\nabla \times v)+v \times(\nabla \times u)$
  7. $\nabla \cdot(u \times v) =v \cdot(\nabla \times u)-u \cdot(\nabla \times v)$
  8. $\nabla \times(u \times v) =u(\nabla \cdot v)-v(\nabla \cdot u)+(v \cdot \nabla) u-(u \cdot \nabla) v$
